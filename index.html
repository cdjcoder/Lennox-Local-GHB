Direct Marketing VS Indirect Marketing Marketing Directo VS Marketing Indirecto

Examples of Direct Marketing
(You control who receives it)

  • Mailed postcards (like our 9x12 flyers)
  • Phone calls (cold or warm leads)
  • Emails (with a known list)
  • Text messages
  • Door-to-door flyers or visits
  • One-on-one video calls
  • In-person networking and tabling at events

These are trackable, measurable, and intentional forms of contact. You're directly engaging a specific person or household.

Examples of Indirect Marketing
(You hope someone sees it)

  • Social media posts or boosted ads
  • Billboard signage
  • Radio ads
  • TV commercials
  • YouTube or streaming service ads
  • Bus stop posters or wraps
  • Airplane banner ads
  • Smoke signals (yes, even that counts!)
  • A message in a bottle floating in the ocean

In other words: you broadcast your message, but you don't know who saw it—or if they did at all.
